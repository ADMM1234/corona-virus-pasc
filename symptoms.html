What are the symptoms of COVID-19?

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate symptoms.

Common symptoms:

  1. fever
  2. tiredness
  3. dry cough

Some people may experience:

  1. aches and pains
  2. nasal congestion
  3. runny nose
  4. sore throat
  5. diarrhoea
